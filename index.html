Craggenrock is a crafting shop management game taking place in a remote island after a great disaster. The great evil has been defeated but monsters are still running amok. As a crafter, take this opportunity to improve your skills and make some money.

  • 200+ materials to experiment with to craft your products
  • Over 700 recipes available to explore for maximum profit
  • Accept requests to craft, repair or upgrade your customer's gear.

Some other things to look out for

  • Shop for seasonal materials as months go by.
  • Care for the environment by recycling junk materials.
  • Watch out for random events that affect the town, the merchants and... your sales!
  • Upgrade your sales license to get more, better-paying (but more meticulous) customers.
