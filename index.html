Some other things to look out for

  • Craft and sell equipment on your workshop with your workstations.
  • Experiment with 200+ materials to craft your products.
  • Explore the 700+ recipes for maximum gold.
  • Accept requests to craft, repair or upgrade your customer's gear.
  • Out of materials? Enter the woodcutter's grocer shop. Order ores and ingots in advance from the mining office. Bargain with the merchants in the market for herbs and monster parts.
  • Issue orders to adventurers to get the items you need.
  • Shop for seasonal materials as months go by.
  • Care for the environment by recycling junk materials.
  • Watch out for random events that affect the town, the merchants and... your sales!
  • Upgrade your sales license to get more, better-paying (but more meticulous) customers.
